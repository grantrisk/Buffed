BUFFED is a calorie-tracking web app built to keep up with your goals. View a colorful breakdown of desired calories on your personalized Dashboard, where you can choose daily meals to stay on track.

When you register for a BUFFED account, all it takes are a few questions to help tailor the experience to fit your goals. We handle calculating the details, and we even set up a standard goal to start you off right away. Feel free to add, customize, and delete old goals as you go.

BUFFED is the new way to balance food and fitness.

  • Browse over 2.3 million recipes in English
  • 500+ top web recipe sources
  • Filter by calories, diet, or allergy restrictions

BUFFED lets you take control of your fitness goals.

  • Sign up takes less than 3 minutes--no long questionnaires
  • Find suggested meals based off of your personalized diet profile
  • Look up and save favorite meals to reuse in your daily plan
  • Edit your Goals, or Create more than one to Flex between
